Solution

How might we reduce the amount of mercury contamination that people in Oakland are exposed to? To solve this dilemma we engineered M.U.S. which stands for Mercury Utensil Set. M.U.S is a utensil set that detects and measures levels of mercury contamination in food.It also spreads awareness about mercury contamination.The utensils each have a way of measuring mercury levels in your food when you cook or eat. M.U.S. provides people with:

  • A spoon
  • A fork
  • A reusable straw
  • A oven-safe meat poker
  • knife with a detachable blade
  • Information pamphlet
  • Instruction manual
